IGEM/UP/1B Edition 4
What changed in March 2026.

IGEM/UP/1B Edition 4 was published in March 2026 and supersedes Edition 3. It's the current standard for tightness testing and purging of domestic and small commercial gas installations in the UK, and all Gas Safe engineers are expected to work to it.

If you're still using Edition 3 figures or memory from your initial training, there's a good chance something has changed. UP/1B Calculator implements Edition 4 in full — so you don't have to track it yourself.

New

Caravans & Light Agricultural Vehicles

Edition 4 extends scope to include caravans and LAVs for the first time, with dedicated test procedures and pressure limits.

Updated

Revised Two-Stage Test Logic

Clarifications to Stage 1 and Stage 2 permissible pressure drop thresholds and how let-by is assessed against installation volume.

Updated

LPG-Air & LPG Procedures

Updated working pressure references and test pressure calculations for LPG and LPG-Air installations.

Updated

Purging Volumes & Sequencing

Refined guidance on purge volumes for different installation sizes and appliance types, covering direct and indirect purging sequences.

New

Documentation Requirements

Strengthened requirements for recording and retaining test results — reinforcing the need for a documented audit trail on every job.

Delete Your Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your UP/1B Calculator account and what happens to your data when you do.

🗑️ How to request account deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Follow these steps:

1

Open the UP/1B Calculator app

Launch the app on your iOS or Android device and make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Profile or Settings icon in the app navigation. This is usually found in the bottom tab bar or the top-right menu.

3

Select "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and tap Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm your decision before anything is deleted.

4

Confirm deletion

Tap Confirm Delete on the confirmation prompt. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

✓  Can't access the app? You can also request account deletion by submitting a request via our support page below. Include the email address associated with your account and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

📋 What data is deleted and what is kept

The following table sets out what happens to each type of data when you request account deletion.

Data type What happens Retention period
Account credentials
Email address, encrypted password		 Deleted Deleted within 30 days of request
Engineer profile
Name, Gas Safe number, business details		 Deleted Deleted within 30 days of request
Test records
Saved tightness test and purging job summaries		 Deleted Deleted within 30 days of request
Support requests
Messages submitted via the support form		 Retained Retained for up to 12 months for operational and legal compliance purposes, then permanently deleted
Purchase records
Transaction data held by Apple or Google		 Retained by Apple / Google Governed by Apple's and Google's own retention policies — we do not hold this data

⚠  Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your test records and profile data cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting any records you wish to keep before submitting a deletion request.

✉️ Request deletion via support

If you are unable to delete your account from within the app, you can submit a deletion request directly to our support team. Please select "Account issue" as the subject and include the email address registered to your account.
